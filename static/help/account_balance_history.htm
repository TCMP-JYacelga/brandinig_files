Account Balance History

Account >> Account Summary >> Account Summary

The Account Balance History  screen enables you to view the history of the balances of the selected Account based on the criteria selected for the Date filter.

To view the Account Balance History screen:

In the Account Balance screen, click the History icon  of the selected 'Account Number'  to open the Account Balance History screen.

You can Filter the search to view the history of the Account Balance of the selected period.

Specify the below criteria:

□         Select Account: Select the account/ IBAN number from the list displaying all the accounts linked to the user.

□         Date: Select the Date for which Account Activity is to be displayed. Default value is 'Today'. Other options are (Yesterday, Current Week, Last Week, Current Month, Last Month, Date Range). Select Date Range and specify the range to view the account activity of a particular period.

On selecting the required filter criteria, click Refresh to view the relevant account balance.

View the total Opening and Closing Available Balance as well as the Opening and Closing Ledger Balance on the selected posting date.

Note:

The Opening / Closing Available and Ledger balances displayed here are the last recorded balances if any of the from and to dates fall on holidays or weekends.

Eg.

Say a User enters

From Date: as 04/03/2012 (Sunday)

To Date: 17/03/2012 (Saturday)

Then the system fetches closing available balance and closing ledger balance as of 03/03/2012 and are displayed against the opening available balance and opening ledger balance fields respectively.

The system fetches the closing balance as of 15/03/2012 and are displayed against the closing available balance and closing ledger balance fields respectively.

 

The detailed Account Balance is further displayed under the following heads:

§        Date: This column indicated the date on which the transaction was hosted on to the application.

Click on the Date hyper link to go to the Account Activity screen to view the movement of your account for the selected day.

§        Opening Available Balance: This column displays the available opening balance of that day.

§        Closing Available Balance: This column displays the available closing balance of that day.

 If amount is displayed in Red color, it indicates Debit Amount.

§        Opening Ledger Balance: This column displays the opening ledger balance of that day.

§        Closing Ledger Balance: This column displays the closing ledger balance of that day.

Click the View Additional Information  icon of a selected transaction as shown below, to open the Additional Information popup which displays further details of the selected transaction. Click OK to close the screen.

Click Export to download the Account Balance History report. The options are:

□         Click  XLS icon to download screen data in XLS format.

□         Click PDF to download data in PDF format.

Click the Back button to go to the Account Balance screen.
