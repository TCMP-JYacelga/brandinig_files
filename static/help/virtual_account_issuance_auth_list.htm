Authorizing Virtual Accounts

The ‘Virtual Account Issuance Auth List’ screen tells you at a glance whether a particular entry has been authorized or not. This is indicated in the Status column, which displays the current state of the corresponding entry.

You can Filter the search by Date range, Client Code, Client Credit Account No. and Issuance Number range to retrieve the accounts pending authorization.

To Authorize a Virtual Account Entry:

1.      Click the Accept check box of the account that you want to authorize. On successful authorization, a message confirming the same will be displayed on the screen. The record moves out of this screen and can be viewed in the ‘Virtual Account Issuance List’ screen with status as Authorized.

Or

Click Reject to reject the request. A popup appears prompting you to enter the ‘Reject Remarks’.

2.      Enter the reason for rejecting the request and click OK.

Note:

-         Click History of an existing Mandate in the Mandate Master which also shows the Maker-Checker history for the last change done on the Mandate definition

-         Click View to view the Mandate and Contact Details of the corresponding  Mandate in the Mandate Entry screen, provided you have access rights to view the particular definition.
