About Collection My Product List

□         In this master you can customize your Collection products as per your client’s requirements. While doing so, you have to map the My Product that you define to the corresponding product in your back office system. Thus, you can customize the product definitions to suit your client’s requirements.

□         All relationships that exist between a My Product and a user or a My Product and an account are based on the products that you define in this master.

To search for a particular My Product, specify the Product Code, Description or Status and click Filter to retrieve the relevant My Product.

IMP:

-         Before you set up My Products, ensure that products have been defined in the Products master in your back office system.

-         Ensure that you select the client for which you want to set up the master definition using the feature- Switch Client

Defining a Collection My Product:

§        Click the Add New Collection My Product button to open the Collection My Product Master Entry screen.

§        The newly defined My Product is added to the Collection My Product List, with its state specified as New and the same can be edited or discarded.

§        To enable the My Product, it is mandatory to authorize it based on the Maker-Checker concept. The checker can view the new definition in the Collection My Product Authorization List screen and authorize the same provided the rights to authorize are assigned to that checker.

§        A successfully authorized My Product definition request can be viewed in the Collection My Product List screen with its status as Authorized.

Note: If you have created My Products that you do not want to enable for further use in the system and if those My Products are not yet authorized, you can Discard them.

 

Editing a My Product:

1.      Click Edit to go to the Collection My Product Master Entry screen where you can edit a previously defined master.

2.      Click Update to save the changes.

Note:

  You can modify the definitions if they are in any of the following states:

-         New : Any new definition is not yet authorized and is in the new state.

-         Modified : Any definition that you have modified and not yet authorized and is in the Modified state.

-         Authorized: The definition that is authorized and is in the Enabled state.

The edit option is not available for the definitions that have the state as Disabled, Disable Request, Enable Request, Disable Request Rejected, and Enable Request Rejected, Submitted, Authorized.

On saving the modifications, the definition state changes to Modified. To enable the definition with the Modified state, the definition has to be authorized.

Viewing a My Product:

1.      Click View to go to the Collection My Product Master View screen where you can view a previously defined master. You cannot modify the definition in this screen.

2.      Click Back to return to the Collection My Product List screen.
