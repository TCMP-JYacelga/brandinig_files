About the Reports Module

The Reports module of GCP CashWeb enables you, the bank’s clients to view as well as schedule a set of reports. These reports provided by GCP CashWeb give you information on various aspects such as performance and throughput, as well as details on charges incurred by you in availing of the cash management services offered by the bank.

GCP CashWeb makes use of a Report Manager (RM) that is connected to the bank’s (Back Office System) BOS. RM is an application that picks up report-specific data from the bank’s BOS, generates reports in the specified format, and delivers the same to you.

RM provides two mechanisms to generate the report:

•    On Request: This functionality allows you to generate the report over the Internet in an online real-time manner.

•    Scheduled Reports: This functionality allows you to specify schedules for the generation and delivery of a given report. This information is passed to RM, which generates the reports as per the specifications in the schedule.

The View Reports screen shows a list of all the reports provided by the system. It also enables you to view reports on-demand as well as to view details of pre-generated reports.

For each report, you are provided with two options, namely On Request and Pre Generated.

§        The On Request option can be used to generate reports on a high priority.

§        The Pre-Generated option allows you to view reports that has been previously generated at a specified frequency.

To access a report on a high priority, click On Request to go to the Generate Reports screen

To access pre-generated reports, click Pregenerated to go to the Pre Generated Reports screen.
