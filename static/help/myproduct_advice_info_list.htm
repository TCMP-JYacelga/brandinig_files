About My Product Advice Information

Advice Information refers to any additional information for a product that may be required to be defined for a transaction. Advice Information field labels defined in this master are available in the transaction initiation screens. At the time of initiating a transactions, entering advice information can be made mandatory.

Topics Covered

•    Defining Advice Information for a My Product

•    Editing Advice Information of a My Product

•    Viewing Advice Info. fields defined for a My Product

 

Note:

-         In this master you can define a maximum of 25 My Product Advice Info fields  that will be available to the client in the transaction screen.

-         Advice information fields  defined for My Product in this master will override those defined by the bank i.e. if advice information fields are  defined at bank end as well as My Product Advice Info. master, then those defined in the My Product Advice info. master will be available to the user for entry in the transaction screen.

You can select a Product from the drop down list and click Filter to retrieve the same.

Defining Advice Information for a My Product:

§        Click the Add button to open the My Product Advice Info. screen where you can add instrument related advice information fields for  the MyProduct combination.

§        The record with the newly defined advice information fields is added to the My Product Advice Info. List, with its state specified as New and the same can be edited or discarded.

§        To enable the My Product Advice Info., it is mandatory to authorize the record based on the Maker-Checker concept. The checker can view the new definition in the My Product Advice Info. Auth screen and authorize the same provided the rights to authorize are assigned to that checker.

§        A successfully authorized My Product Advice Info. field definition can be viewed in the My Product Advice Info. List screen with its status as Authorized.

Note: If you have created My Products advice information field definition that you do not want to enable for further use in the system and if those My Products are not yet authorized, you can Discard it.

 

Editing Advice Information of a My Product:

1.      Click Edit to go to the My Product Advice Info. screen where you can edit a previously defined record.

2.      Click Update to save the changes.

Note:

  You can modify the definitions if they are in any of the following states:

-         New : Any new definition is not yet authorized and is in the new state.

-         Modified : Any definition that you have modified and not yet authorized and is in the Modified state.

-         Authorized: The definition that is authorized and is in the Enabled state.

The edit option is not available for the definitions that have the state as Disabled, Disable Request, Enable Request, Disable Request Rejected, and Enable Request Rejected, Submitted, Authorized.

On saving the modifications, the definition state changes to Modified. To enable the definition with the Modified state, the definition has to be authorized.

Viewing Advice Info. fields defined for a My Product:

1.      Click View to go to the My Product Advice Info. screen where you can view a previous definition. You cannot modify the definition in this screen.

2.      Click Back to return to the My Product Advice Info. List screen.
