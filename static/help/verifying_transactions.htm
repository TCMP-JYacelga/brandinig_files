Verifying Transactions

Transaction Verification is an additional feature available for Payment Transactions.

Once a transaction is successfully submitted, system will validate the ‘Verification Required’ field for the derived product in the Payment MyProduct master, and the Product Account linkage, and make the transaction available to the Verifier for authorization. At this stage the status of the transaction is Pending Verification, or Pending My Verification.  The right to verify transactions is assigned by the bank to only ONE user and is done at the same level as defined in Authorization Level field in the Payment MyProduct master, i.e. in case Authorization Level is set to Batch or Batch-Max Instrument amount, then Verification will be at Batch level else at Instrument level. In case the user is assigned Across Entity rights for a corporation, transactions can be verified for clients spread across different countries.

Once the transaction is successfully verified, it goes into AVM/SVM workflow for further processing. In case the verifier rejects the transaction, it goes to the maker queue for editing.

Post verification, the status of the transaction becomes 'Pending Auth' or 'Pending My Auth'.

To Verify a Transaction:

1    In the Payment center, retrieve the transactions with status as Pending My Verification.

2    Select the transaction(s) that you want to verify.

3    Click to verify the transactions.

OR

Click ‘More’ and select Reject or Discard in case you want to reject or delete the transaction.
